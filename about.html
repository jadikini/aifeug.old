About Us

OUR VISSION

An Africa resilient to climate change and utilizes its natural resources sustainably.

OUR MISSION

To eradicate food insecurity and environmental destruction in Africa.

Objectives

  • To report on the effects of climate change to food production and distribution.
  • To educate communinities on effective means of adoptation and mitigation of climate change.
  • To contribute to policy formulation on food security and environmental conservation.
  • To support urban farming initiatives.

OUR APPROACH

We organize the ordinary person in the community to be proactive against climate change through community mobilization, teaching, and evaluation of practices while working hand in hand with government and development partners. We shall work heavily with the media for agenda setting and mobilization.

Why We exist

African Initiative for Food Security and Environment (AIFE) is a nonprofit organization in Uganda established for two main objectives;

1. To combat activity that results into hostile, adverse and undesirable effects of climate change while advancing and enhancing activity that results into friendly, favorable and desirable climate conditions; and any other activity incidental thereto which includes but is not limited to the intention of fostering this objective. The foregoing objective’s implementation includes but is not limited to sustainable use of water resources, air space and green layer, land, indigenous foods, flora, fauna and wildlife among others.

2. To establish, run and operate projects, programmes as well as conduct research and such other arrangements intended to cause and effect friendly, favorable and desirable climate change while combating activity that results into hostile, adverse and undesirable effects of climate conditions; and any other activity incidental thereto which includes but is not limited to the intention of fostering this objective.
