ONGOING PROJECTS

THE GREEN CITY PROJECT

In this project we are training urban families how to practice backyard gardening, indoor farming in their kitchens and vertical farming to solve urban inequality, hunger and poverty to urban populations solving sewage and flooding issues by treating wastewater for re-use in the gardens hence developing greener cities for recreation, leisure, economic vitality and business entrepreneurship. Our main operations area is Kampala and major Towns across Uganda at the moment

WATER IN THE CORRIDOR

This project involves water harvesting and sustainable use of the water resource in the cattle corridor. These include rain water harvesting and maintenance, building of dams with local communities using locally available materials. Currently our main areas of operation is largely Western Uganda and North Eastern Uganda in Karamoja region where majority farmers are pastralists and have suffered drought over time
